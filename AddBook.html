Our Events
Reader's Marathon:

Description: The goal is to read the maximum number of pages or books in a short period of time.

Time: On the last Saturday of the month, from 10:00 to 18:00

Thematic Meetings:

Description: Discussion of books of a certain genre or topic (for example, science fiction, classics, historical novels).

Time: Every Friday at 19:00.

Master classes in Literary Creativity:

Description: Teaching writing skills, creating your own texts, discussing them with other participants.

Time: Every Thursday at 17:30.

Literary Readings:

Description: Discussion and exchange of impressions about the current book of the week.

Time: Every Wednesday at 18:00
